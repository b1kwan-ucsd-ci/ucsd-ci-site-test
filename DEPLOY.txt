UC San Diego CI Fellowship — Password-protected preview
=======================================================

WHAT THIS IS
  The five pages in this folder are AES-encrypted. Nobody can read the
  content without the password, even though the files are hosted publicly.
  Password: ucsdcisite

DEPLOY ON GITHUB PAGES (free, no other accounts)

  STEP 1 - CREATE THE REPOSITORY
    a. Sign in at github.com (create a free account if you don't have one).
    b. Click the "+" icon at the top-right -> "New repository".
    c. Repository name: ci-fellowship-preview  (any name is fine).
    d. Leave it set to "Public". (Pages is free on public repos. The files
       are encrypted, so public hosting is safe.)
    e. Do NOT check "Add a README" - keep it empty.
    f. Click "Create repository".

  STEP 2 - UPLOAD THE FILES
    a. First, UNZIP the file you downloaded so you can see the loose files
       (the 5 .html files, image-slot.js, DEPLOY.txt, and the assets folder).
    b. On the new empty repo page, find the line "uploading an existing file"
       and click it.  (Or click "Add file" -> "Upload files".)
    c. Open the unzipped "preview" folder on your computer, select ALL of its
       contents (Ctrl+A / Cmd+A), and DRAG them onto the upload area in the
       browser.  IMPORTANT: drag the CONTENTS of the folder, not the folder
       itself - the .html files must sit at the top level of the repo, with
       the "assets" folder beside them.  GitHub preserves the assets/ subfolder
       automatically.
    d. Wait for every file to finish uploading (you'll see them listed,
       including "assets/..." entries).
    e. Scroll down to "Commit changes" and click the green "Commit changes"
       button.

  STEP 3 - TURN ON GITHUB PAGES
    a. In the repo, click the "Settings" tab (top-right of the repo menu).
    b. In the left sidebar, click "Pages".
    c. Under "Build and deployment" -> "Source", choose "Deploy from a branch".
    d. Under "Branch", change "None" to "main".  Leave the folder as "/ (root)".
    e. Click "Save".

  STEP 4 - GET YOUR LINK
    a. Stay on the Pages settings screen and wait about 1-2 minutes.
    b. Refresh the page. A banner appears: "Your site is live at
       https://<your-username>.github.io/ci-fellowship-preview/".
    c. Click "Visit site" to test it - you should see the password screen.
       Enter the password (ucsdcisite) to confirm the site loads.
    d. Share that URL and the password with your reviewers.

  UPDATING LATER
    To change content or the password, ask me to regenerate the preview,
    then repeat STEP 2 (Add file -> Upload files) to replace the files.

NOTES

    type it once while clicking between pages.
